Comp. Scientist mayor. Former scientist and biologist

Dont panic, its only making it worse!

As Luka Susha Jr. stated in the previous interview with the global health authorities. Its important that your hygiene is well maintained. Also people have to minimalize their contact with other people. To have the smallest chance to get the COVID-19 Its important to stay at home.

Things to keep track of:

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Dont touch your face
  3. Sneeze onto your elbow
  4. Dont do handshakes
  5. Minimalize contact with others

Do not fall for scams.

There are no special masks for corona virus.

Masks are used by ill people so they cannot spread the virus to others. Civilans dont have any benefit of wearing a mask, its only making it harder for them to breathe. Also there is no cure for the disease and if someone is trying to sell you one its a scam.
